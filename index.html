Driving Simulation Lab – Cognitive Science

Exploring how human cognition shapes driving behavior

Introduction

Our Driving Simulation Lab studies driving behaviors and their connection to cognitive science. We use advanced simulators to understand how people think, perceive, and make decisions while driving.

About the Lab

We focus on experimental and applied research related to driver behavior, attention, perception, and safety. Our work supports better road systems and human-centered vehicle technologies.

Research Goals

  • Study driver attention and distraction
  • Understand decision-making under traffic conditions
  • Analyze human factors in driving performance
  • Improve road safety through cognitive research
